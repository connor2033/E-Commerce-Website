About Us


Welcome to Haines Sports & Athletics, and thanks for visiting our online store! Here at Haines S&A, we believe that staying healthy and fit is key, and what better way to achieve that than sports! Here you'll find products from basketballs and hockey skates to row boats, and much, much more. If you don't find what you're looking for and you'd like to contact us, please can fill out our contact form located here. We're sure you'll have a great experience and we look forward to meeting any of your needs. Thanks for shopping at Haines Sports & Athletics!
